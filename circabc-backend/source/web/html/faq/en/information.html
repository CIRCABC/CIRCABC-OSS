  • How to customize your information pages

    First create your website with any websites editor (preferably not Word),
    Go to the Information service, Browse repository,
    Upload your web pages using the Add Content or Bulk Upload

    Create a space where you upload the images relating to the html file loaded in ‘content’ (similar to ‘images_files folder’ of your website)


    Then configure the index page (Welcome page of your information service) : From the Administration page of your Information service, Modify Information Service properties
