CIRCABC (Collaborative and Information Resources Centre for Administration, Businesses and Citizens) provides a web-based application, which enables geographically spread collaborative groups to share documents, information and resources in private workspace.

CIRCABC is accessible to any user inside or outside of the European Institutions. Businesses; international, national or local administrations; and associations of citizens can access and use CIRCABC.

The platform

CIRCABC was launched January 2006 by the European Commission and funded by the European Union via the Interoperability solutions for public administrations, businesses and citizens (ISA²) Programme. Thanks to this service, you will be able to share documents with colleagues working in the European Union Institutions and with third parties collaborators.

The provided platform is divided into categories and interest groups, allowing people to manage content, users and communications in a more organised way.

It offers functionalities such as:

  • multilingual and multi-format content (any file/document), with version control;
  • high level of security, as the system uses the HTTPS protocol to guarantee confidential data transfer;
  • secured access by EU Login, the European Commission’s Authentication Service;
  • high-availability environment;
  • fine-grained access management, depending on the user’s access profile;
  • sharing of users and information between different workspaces;
  • translation management, multilingual search;
  • scheduling events and meetings;
  • discussion forums;
  • accessibility for users with disabilities (WAI compliancy).

Can you speak CIRCABC?

The application is divided into Categories and Interest Groups, allowing people in those groups to share information and resources.

CIRCABC calls an INTEREST GROUP (IG) the private workspace shared by a community of users and a CATEGORY is a set of Interest Groups. Some Interest Groups may be entirely or partly public (no authentication required). Others can be entered by any logged-in user ("registered access"). Others are reserved for their members.

An Interest Group is characterized by a group of users (potentially unlimited), a set of services (Information, Library, Members, Events, Forums, etc.) and a series of contents.

Users may be granted more or less rights depending on their access profile.

What is set of services included?

  • Information service :
    This functionality allows you to publish articles, news and any kind of information within your group.
  • Library service :
    This functionality allows you to upload, share and manage documents in a folders-structured way. CIRCABC allows for multilingual and multi-format content (any file/document), with version control and automated translation.
  • Members :
    This functionality allows you to manage the members of your group and classify them into profiles with specific customisable access rights.
  • Agenda/Meeting :
    This functionality provides you a group calendar, allowing the organization of meetings (with audience) or events and to directly link documents from the Library.
  • Forums :
    This functionality provides you with discussion fora, allowing the free and easy exchange of ideas between the members of the group.
  • Administration :
    This functionality provides Administrator use to manage the Interest Group. Security : The system provides a very fine-grained permission mechanism allowing a flexible group security management (e.g. visibility of the group, access rights for ‘Profiles’, ‘Members’, per service, folder, or file)
